# LUNDAI — Product Strategy & Pricing for Malawi

This strategy outlines a sustainable business model for LUNDAI within the Malawian student housing market, balancing affordability with operational growth.

## 1. Market Insight (Malawi)
-   **Student Demographics**: Low income, high price sensitivity. Reliance on mobile money (Airtel Money, TNM Mpamba).
-   **Landlords**: Individual homeowners (non-professional) to small-scale hostel owners. High WhatsApp usage.
-   **Pain Point**: Scams on Facebook Marketplace and high "agent fees" for students searching for rooms.

## 2. Freemium Pricing Model

All payments processed via **PayChangu** to support local mobile money options.

### Tier 1: Free (Standard Listing)
-   **Target**: Individual landlords with 1-2 rooms.
-   **Price**: **0 MWK**
-   **Features**:
    -   Standard search appearance.
    -   Up to 4 images.
    -   Direct inquiry via WhatsApp.
    -   Basic verification badge.

### Tier 2: Premium (Featured Listing)
-   **Target**: High-demand hostels or landlords seeking quick occupancy.
-   **Price**: **5,000 MWK per week** / **15,000 MWK per month**
-   **Features**:
    -   **Featured Status**: Appears at the top of search results.
    -   **AI Priority**: Highlighted in AI-powered natural language searches.
    -   Up to 10 high-resolution images.
    -   "LUNDAI Verified Preferred" badge.

### Tier 3: Agent / Manager Plan
-   **Target**: Property managers, hostel companies, or universities.
-   **Price**: **45,000 MWK per month**
-   **Features**:
    -   Unlimited property listings.
    -   Performance Analytics (View counts, inquiry counts).
    -   WhatsApp Webhook Integration for automated ingestion.
    -   Listing Management Dashboard.

## 3. Revenue Acceleration (Micro-Transactions)
-   **Inquiry Credits**: Students may "Unlock" premium landlord contact info for a small fee (**500 MWK**) if using the free tier (model to be tested).
-   **Verification Service**: Landlords pay **10,000 MWK** for a one-time physical site visit by LUNDAI and a "Physical Site Verified" badge to increase trust.

## 4. Growth Strategy
1.  **University Partners**: Collaborate with UNIMA, MUST, and LUARAR to link LUNDAI in their orientation guides.
2.  **WhatsApp "Bot" Growth**: Incentivize landlord listing via the WhatsApp ingestion service for low-bandwidth users.
3.  **Campus Ambassadors**: Commission-based students who verify and register local homes on the platform.
