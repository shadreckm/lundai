# LUNDAI Marketplace Launch & Growth Strategy

LUNDAI is an AI-powered housing discovery platform starting in Mzuzu, Malawi. This strategy focuses on solving the cold-start problem by dominating the Mzuzu University student housing market before expanding nationwide.

## 1. 90-Day Launch Plan

### Days 1-30: The Supply Sprint (Phase 1)
*   **Objective**: Secure 50-100 high-quality listings around Mzuzu University.
*   **Actions**:
    *   Deploy 5 "Supply Scouts" to walk the housing areas (Area 2, Area 1B, Luwinga).
    *   Onboard landlords manually using the "2-Minute Quick Listing" tool.
    *   Offer "LUNDAI Verified" status for free to the first 50 landlords.
    *   Professional photography service: Scouts take photos for landlords to ensure high listing quality.

### Days 31-60: The Demand Surge (Phase 2 & 3)
*   **Objective**: Acquire 500-1,000 active student users.
*   **Actions**:
    *   Launch "Campus Ambassador" program with 10 influential MZUNI students.
    *   Seed listings into 50+ MZUNI WhatsApp groups and Facebook communities.
    *   Enable "AI Scam Detection" and "Verified" badges to build immediate trust.
    *   Host a "Find Your Room" event on campus with free Wi-Fi for app browsing.

### Days 61-90: Viral Optimization (Phase 4)
*   **Objective**: Achieve a K-factor > 1 (every user brings at least one more).
*   **Actions**:
    *   Launch "Refer a Roommate" rewards (e.g., airtime or data bundles).
    *   Implement "Saved Search Alerts" via SMS/In-app for high-demand areas.
    *   Incentivize WhatsApp sharing with "Unlock Contact" discounts.

## 2. Landlord Acquisition Strategy
*   **Hyper-Local Presence**: Physical presence in Mzuzu housing clusters is non-negotiable.
*   **Zero-Friction Onboarding**: Landlords often use low-end smartphones; the listing flow must be lightweight and require minimal typing (AI-assisted descriptions).
*   **Value Proposition**: "Stop answering 100 phone calls; let LUNDAI filter the serious students for you."
*   **Early Adopter Perks**: 6 months of free "Featured" status for the first 20 landlords who maintain a 100% response rate.

## 3. Student Growth Strategy
*   **The "WhatsApp First" Approach**: Students live on WhatsApp. Every listing must be "one-tap shareable" to WhatsApp groups.
*   **Ambassador Incentives**: Commission-based rewards for every verified student they bring to the platform who completes a profile.
*   **Trust as a Feature**: Students in Mzuzu fear scams. Highlight "Verified Photos" and "Landlord Identity Check" as the primary reason to use LUNDAI over Facebook Marketplace.

## 4. Viral Growth Mechanisms
*   **WhatsApp Share Loops**: "Share this listing with 3 friends to unlock the landlord's direct phone number for free."
*   **Roommate Matching**: Students often look for rooms in pairs. The "Invite a Roommate" feature creates a natural viral loop.
*   **Price Drop Alerts**: When a landlord lowers the price, all users who "Saved" the listing get an instant notification, encouraging them to share the "deal" with their network.

## 5. Key Metrics to Monitor
*   **Liquidity Ratio**: Number of inquiries per listing (Target: > 5).
*   **Trust Conversion**: Percentage of users who only interact with "Verified" listings vs. unverified.
*   **Viral Coefficient**: Number of new users acquired via referral links/shares.
*   **Retention**: Percentage of students who return to the app within 7 days of their first search.
